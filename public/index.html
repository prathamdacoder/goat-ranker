What is GOAT Ranker?

GOAT Ranker is a real-time community voting platform where users decide who or what is truly the Greatest Of All Time. Vote for top YouTubers like MrBeast and IShowSpeed, rank the best drinks like Prime, or add anything you want the world to rank. Every vote counts and the leaderboard updates live.

How it works

1. Add anything you want ranked.
2. Click the 🔺 button to upvote your favorites.
3. Watch the leaderboard update in real-time.
4. Share with friends and let the community decide the GOAT.
